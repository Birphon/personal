Anomalies

Anomalies are formations of an unknown nature that appeared in the Zone after the second explosion. They represent a great danger to the health and life of any living creature, but they form an artifact, which makes many stalkers investigate them.

Biochemical

Kissel

A dangerous anomaly of a biochemical nature. It looks like a luminous bright green liquid, clearly visible in the dark. Upon contact with the body, it inflicts injuries similar to the effect of exposure to a strong acid. In a suit with a closed breathing cycle, damage will be minimal. In this anomaly, strange chemical processes take place non-stop. Their result is an acid that corrodes the skin, and poisonous gases that corrode the lungs with equal success. Even in passive mode, the anomaly produces a dim green light and is easy to spot even in the dark. When interacting with any object, it hisses strongly, so it can also be detected with bolts.

Thermal

Frying

In the inactive state, it looks like a barely visible cloud of hot air, but when it enters the zone of action of any object or living being, it forms a compact zone that looks like a jet of fire and is heated to a temperature of about 1500K (1227 Â° C). The frying itself is a jet of burning gas. The anomaly is activated when any object hits, so it can be detected by throwing bolts both day and night.

Steam

Thermal anomaly. It is a jet of red-hot thick white steam escaping from the ground. The anomaly is active all the time, so it is easily detected and does not pose a serious danger.

The Cirus

A complex anomaly of a thermal nature, which is a circle with a diameter of about ten meters, along which several so-called "Comets", or wandering fries, are circling. In the center is constantly active frying. Perhaps the anomaly is also of a mental nature - sometimes Comets do not burn a creature that gets in their way, do not produce heat, and are not fixed by scientific equipment. This hints that some "Comets" may be a collective hallucination of observers. The anomaly is active all the time, so it is easy to detect. However, her damage radius can change, so it's best to avoid her.

Electrophysical

Elektra

An anomalous formation of an electrical nature with a diameter of about three meters, accumulating static electricity. A disturbed anomaly explodes with a dozen mini-lightnings, and an electric shock for any living creature is almost always fatal. A characteristic feature of the "Electra" is the bluish light visible above it during the day. The anomaly is easily distinguishable at any time of the day: during the day it can be identified by blue fog and small lightning, sometimes slipping through the air, and at night - by a glow or a throw of a metal object. You can also hear characteristic crackling sounds.

Gravitational

Black Hole

An anomaly of presumably gravitational nature. At the moment of activation, with terrible force, it draws in everything that is within a radius of three to four meters. If it hits the center of the anomaly, there is no chance of survival: the body of both the animal and the person will be compressed into a tight lump, and then torn apart at the moment of the so-called discharge. Does not change the place of its manifestation. An anomaly can be visually detected during the day by the characteristic movement of air above it and swirling foliage. It is extremely dangerous at night, as it is detected only by throwing metal objects.

Vortex

Gravitational anomaly; lifts the victim into the air, spins it and causes severe physical damage. Survivors after falling into this anomaly say that the anomaly seemed to "periodically beat them with a large blunt object." It is very important not to miss the moment of the beginning of being drawn into the anomalous vortex and not to fall into the zone of the strongest effect in the center - only then there is a chance to get off with minimal injuries. Detected by a light dust whirlwind. Most likely, the anomaly does not react to inanimate objects. If you throw a bolt into the Vortex, then a rainbow glare will appear at the boundary of the impact, and the bolt will bounce a certain distance.

Trampoline

One of the first recorded anomalies. Inflicts impact damage with an alternating gravity field. The result of a meeting with a "trampoline" can be different - from small bruises and bruises to instant death. In the daytime, it is quite easily detected by the distortion of the air above it. It is fixed with the help of various objects thrown into it.

Nascent Anomalies

This is a special type of anomaly that can only be detected using the SAK-1 or SAK-2 wide range detectors. The main purpose of such anomalies is to extract artifacts from them using special anomalous installations. As well as ready-made anomalies, there are four types, and therefore the corresponding artifacts fall out of them. Mirror

The anomaly has not been fully explored. Upon impact, the subject begins to tear from the inside with every movement. "As if the guy had been smashed like a mirror" - Beard.
This anomaly is found only in "Limansk" and is poorly visible at any time of the day. When hit in the "Mirror", an effect appears on the screen that disappears over time.

Stagnation

A cloudy hemisphere, imperceptible at rest and significantly slowing down all processes in the human body that has fallen into it. Delay in the anomaly results in death. In the inactive state, it can be detected by a slight wave distortion emanating from the center.
