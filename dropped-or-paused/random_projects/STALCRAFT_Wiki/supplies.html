Supplies

Trade backpacks - a special STALCRAFT game mechanic In many bases, you can find a Supplier NPC who will offer you to buy a backpack with unknown contents and deliver it to the recipient. When you buy a backpack, it is equipped, and the only way to get rid of it is to deliver it, throw it away or lose it. As a reward for the delivery, you will receive reputation points, money, barter resources.

Daily Quests - a special STALCRAFT game mechanic In many bases, you can find a Quest Board. A number of quests will be shown with the return values. As a reward for the delivery, you will receive reputation points, money, barter resources

It is important to know

  • The backpack drops when you die and exit the game, so you have to be very careful and repel any attack. You can ask your friends to help you, find like-minded people to deliver the goods.
  • The player who picks up someone else's cargo and delivers it to the nearest supplier receives a reward 2 times less than due to the player who bought this cargo, for this they also give an achievement called "Gop-stop" .
  • On the lost backpack, the nickname of the one who carried the backpack is indicated, so if you lost it, you can easily find it.
  • Contracts for backpacks are updated once a day.
  • A contract that is too complex can be canceled and then it will appear after 16 hours.
  • With the improvement of the reputation at the base, more complex delivery contracts are opened.
