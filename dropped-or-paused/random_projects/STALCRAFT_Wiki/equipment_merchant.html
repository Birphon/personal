Equipment Merchant

Equipment Merchant - NPC found in all allied locations; this NPC is one of four ways to get equipment after the rebuild (purchase from the Equipment Merchant, purchase with donation, purchase from other players, receive as a reward for the quest)

The assortment of the merchant depends on the location: the further north it is, the better the assortment, the easiest and cheapest loot is in the Swamp, the best is in the 100 Roentgen Bar. To purchase each item, you need rubles and barter items (except for the second, some equipment in the Swamps). All you need to trade is:

  • Rubles
  • Barter items from digs
  • Barter items from human NPC spots and patrols
  • Barter items from mutant NPC spots
  • Barter items from anomalous installations
It is worth considering that each location has its own merchant, for example, in the Swamps it is Petka (for the stalker faction) and Barbara (for the bandit faction), but in the 100 X-ray Bar it is Curie. Faction Traders are the most advanced Traders at the moment
