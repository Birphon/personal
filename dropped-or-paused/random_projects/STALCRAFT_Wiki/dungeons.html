Dungeons

Dungeons (also Dungeons, from the English Dungeon) are special locations, inside which are in the form of anomalies , mutants and elite NPC's . Dungeons are '''not connected''' with the open world, so you can be in the dungeon either alone or as part of a squad (but note that there is only one cash reward for passing, which means the more players in the squad, the less money each participant will receive For example, if there are 4 people in the squad, then the reward is divided by 4). You also won't see other players going through the same dungeon at the same time. Each location has from one dungeon to several, differing in difficulty and reward for completing this dungeon. Next will be a table.or navigation beacon . There are currently no dungeons in the game.
