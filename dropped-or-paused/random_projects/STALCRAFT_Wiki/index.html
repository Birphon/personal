What is STALCRAFT

This is a time-tested MMO shooter in the open world of the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone.
The world of the game is saturated with stalker romance and will not leave any fan of the STALKER trilogy indifferent, because the same fans of the series have been lovingly developing the project for 7 years.
Initially, STALCRAFT was conceived only as a fan-made global modification to Minecraft. Over the years of development, the project has changed significantly, and is now based on its own game engine.
