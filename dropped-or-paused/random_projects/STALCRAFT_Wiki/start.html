Beginning of the game

Chernobyl NPP

Pioneer Prince, wakes up the player in the role of a stalker Voice through the radio. The Wounded Voice gets up and begins his adventure through the power plant. The prince tells where the player is, and also instructs to get to the evacuation point with the Blue Ball artifact. Going through the entire construction, the Prince will give advice and teach the player various easy features. In the beginning, you will heal, run up the stairs and find a sniper position. The player will be given an SVD and an order to destroy the Monoliths on the roof. At the end of the shooting, the player is given the coordinates of the corpse with APS and AKS-74u . Then there is climbing on the Fourth Power Unit and shooting from various weapons.

Tips

  • Don't forget to search the corpses. Both the submachine gun and the PKP "Pecheneg" can fall out of the corpses .
  • The buggers have medicines. If suddenly you are hit hard, but there are no medicines, return to the old points and collect first-aid kits and bandages. If there is nowhere to retreat, it is advised to find good shelter from bullets.
