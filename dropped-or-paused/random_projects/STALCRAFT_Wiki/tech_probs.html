Technical problems

Below are the most common problems with the game and the launcher. If your problem is not listed, you can leave a comment at the end of the article or contact yourself here.

The "OpenJDK" program has stopped working.

There are a lot of reasons for this problem: lack of system resources, namely video memory or RAM, damaged game files, incorrectly installed drivers for the video card, too high graphics settings that do not fit the system resources of the computer.
Possible ways to solve this problem:
  1. Press the key combination "Win" + "R", in the window that appears, enter the command "msconfig", in the configuration menu, find the "Startup" tab (in Windows 8 and 10 programs are disabled in the task manager) and disable all programs that run when you start your operating system. After disabling all programs, restart your computer;
  2. Go to "Programs and Features", remove all unnecessary and virus programs that can only be removed. It is recommended to get rid of all antivirus programs. Manually set the correct swap file size. Don't forget to check all disks for various junk files and check them for errors, don't forget to delete temporary files and empty the recycle bin;
  3. Check your computer with Malwarebytes Anti-Malware . Quarantine all found threats and restart the computer, after that you can disable the antivirus or remove it altogether so that it does not consume system resources, which are not very numerous anyway;
  4. That's right, through the official website or the official program, update the drivers for your video card . Under no circumstances should you use third-party driver updaters, they install drivers that are not for your hardware. Remember, Device Manager cannot update drivers correctly, you need to do it manually;
  5. In the EXBO Launcher settings, select the maximum amount of RAM;
  6. In the game settings, in the "Performance" tab, disable multithreading, and also set the loading of chunks to standard;
  7. In the game settings, in the "Graphics Settings" tab, unscrew all the parameters to the very minimum, be sure to turn off all vegetation.

Rainbow and black and pink textures.

Often, this problem occurs due to incorrectly installed drivers for the video card, in rare cases, it occurs due to lack of video memory, low power, or a malfunction of the video card itself.
Possible ways to solve this problem:
  • Updating your GPU drivers
      Follow the necessary steps to update your GPU drivers. There you can find Instructions:
    • Updating NVIDIA drivers
    • Updating AMD drivers
    • Updating INTEL drivers
  • Cleaning the video card from dust and dirt, replacing the thermal paste on the graphics chip
  • Replacing the video card with a newer and more powerful one.
  • Switching the video card to discrete
