    • Player approaches Kansif (a stoic, honourable male half-orc) and Brawler Vez (a mouthy female goblin) after clearing a checkpoint and entering their camp in the Shadowcurse. They two Absolute cultists assume you're a True Soul sent by Minthara (which may or may not be true) and will ask if you brought the lyre needed to summon their guide through the darkness. If you have, you can play it and the drider will emerge. If you haven't, they can tell you where to find it.
