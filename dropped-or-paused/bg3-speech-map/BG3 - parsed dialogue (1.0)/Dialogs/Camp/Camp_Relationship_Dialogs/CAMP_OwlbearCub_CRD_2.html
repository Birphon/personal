    In SwA, the owlbear cub speaks in short staccato 'sentences.' He's vicious and feral - but scared. He's slightly injured, and playing it up more than it is, and he's distrustful of the player at first. If the player helps him, he becomes excited and runs off to hunt. In the next night, he returns with a dead bird.

    The owlbear has appeared in camp for a second night. The player has previously spared the cub after killing its mother, and saved it from a cruel game run by the goblins in their camp. He reacts much more suspiciously of the player if they antagonised him in their first encounter.
