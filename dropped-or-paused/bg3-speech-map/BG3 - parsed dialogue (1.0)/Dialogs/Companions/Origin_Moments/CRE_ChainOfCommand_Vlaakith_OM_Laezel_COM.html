In both cases, the planecaster is activated.If you refuse, Vlaakith orders the inquisitor if alive to kill you if you try to exit the room or theatens to hunt you down if he's already dead. If Laezel is not yet convinced to side with the player, they have a chance to do it here if they refuse.
