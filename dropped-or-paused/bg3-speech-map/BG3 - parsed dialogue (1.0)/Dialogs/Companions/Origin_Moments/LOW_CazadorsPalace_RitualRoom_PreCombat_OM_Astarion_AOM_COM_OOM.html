    • Cazador greets Companion or Avatar Astarion in the ritual room, pleased Astarion has returned and he'll finally be able to complete the ritual. The other 6 vampire spawn are in their places for the ritual, ringing the room, held in spectral chains. Astarion starts nervous, but gets angrier as Cazador goads him until Astarion attacks. Cazador uses his magic to immobilise Astarion and then telekinetically flings him into his place (a spot directly behind Cazador) for the ritual, which then begins as the dialog ends and combat starts.
