    Second half - she's weary but recovering.

    Thulla is a member of the Ironhand Gnomes, a group of militant gnomish artificers who came to the Underdark to find a forbidden - and extremely secret - formula for runepowder: an explosive of awesome power.

    Thulla is a true believer in the cause - when her group was captured by duergar, she escaped but picked up a poisoned wound on the way. She's slowly succumbing to poison - initially vague and feverish. If we heal her, she's exhausted but straight back on task, giving us the job of freeing her people.
