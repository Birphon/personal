    Players have been captured by the goblin priestess and could not escape their chains. After they rest, the priestess returns to see if they've transformed into mind flayers (as she expected) and is disappointed to see they haven't. She intends to kill them, but a warlock intervenes and saves the players, killing the priestess. The warlock has been sent by its master, a cambion called The Monitor, who players may have met already.
