    • This dialog will play in 3 different circumstances.
    1) Players unlocked the abductee stealthily before the dialog with Orin. Which would trigger a dialog with Orin. After that dialog with Orin, the abductee will wake up and play this AD. Combat than begins. Yenna would run away, companions will join players as a follower.

    2) Players have entered combat with Orin, and during combat, they unlock the abductee. In which case they will awaken immediately and play this AD. Yenna runs away, companion joins as follower.

    3) Player have defeated Orin and unlock the altar, in which case they are all in relatively safe enviroment. Yenna would not need to run away, companion would not need to join as follower.

    During combat, players attempt to rescue the victim. When the players unlock the altar, the victim is awakened and plays this AD as they are awakened.

    If the victim is a player companion, they join combat as an AI NPC.
    If the victim is Yenna she runs away.
