    • Orin, Chosen of Bhaal resides in Bhaal Temple, waiting for the players to arrive. She has given them an ultimatum to kill Gortash and retrieve his Netherstone for her, or the player's abducted companion will be sacrified. If the players didn't have a valid companion for kidnapping, Yenna will take the place of sacrifice.

    Depending on the approach the player took to the altar, they may be in a slightly different position relative to the altar. Orin will teleport into the same spot on the alter in order to begin the dialogue.

    Regardless of the player's actions within the temple, and regardless of Gortash's fate, Orin will eventually begin a fight with the players. Determined to prove to Bhaal and herself that she is a worthy Chosen of Bhaal.

    She will transform into the slayer in the final moments of the dialogue, and then combat begins.
