Addition of renewable sources for a series of resoruces in the game.
With this also comes modifications that can be applied to vehicles and generators.

BioFuel
    - A replacement for when Gas Pumps and Vehicles run out
        - Allowing for the likes of Little to No Gas settings to be enabled
    - Processing of Corn to make Ethonal
    - BioFuel Conversion (Vehicle)
    - BioFuel Conversion (Generators)

Hemp
