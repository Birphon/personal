Hello and welcome to my Palworld Food Table. This is sorted by the Sanity Gain.

A potential update couple add adding another column for the effects of food as some of them have two or more effects and then have a sort by that as well
Persoanlly, from this finding Carbonara is the best food for your bases as, like mentioned above, it has the effect of Eating it makes it harder for sanity to decrease and improves Defense by +10% for a certain amount of time.
Now, Pizza is technically a better food, however, Pizza does not have the Sanity Decrease, it only has Eating it improves Defense by +15% temporarily.

Sadly, personally I would like to consider the Meat options within the game, however, due to the lack of benificial farming ways for meat as we only have

  • Mass catching then butchering
  • Mass breeding then butchering

which are all rather time consuming tasks, I personally decided to opt out of the Meat options, hence my choice in Carbonara.
